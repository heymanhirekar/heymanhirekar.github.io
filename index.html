About
Who I am

I'm an MBA student at IIM Ranchi with a strong interest in how businesses improve execution, design better systems, and make sharper decisions. My work sits at the intersection of operations, strategy, finance, and analytics, with a growing focus on practical problem-solving.

I've worked across warehouse operations, layout improvement, process analysis, SOP thinking, and broader discussions around planning and platform strategy. Working on the ground has strengthened my belief that good strategy matters only when it can actually be executed well.

I created this website to bring together my projects, reflections, and writing in one place. It is both a portfolio of work and a space for ideas.
