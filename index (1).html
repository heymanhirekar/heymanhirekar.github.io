About

A personal introduction to how I think, what I am working toward, and the kinds of questions that keep me engaged.

I am a management student at IIM Ranchi with strong interest in business thinking that connects theory with execution. I am especially drawn to systems, decision-making, and the practical side of how organizations operate and improve.

This website serves as both a portfolio and an intellectual notebook. It brings together my projects, reflections, and media so that my work and ideas can live in one coherent place.

I want the site to evolve over time into a space where I can publish editorials, short video explainers, and project-based insights that reflect what I am learning.
